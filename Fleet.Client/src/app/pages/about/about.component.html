Fleet Manager

Requirements Implemented:

  • 1. Create containers and load them to ships and trucks.
  • 2. Persist load of ships between service restarts.
  • 3. Restrict ship load to a max of 4 containers.
  • 4. Register an unlimited number of ships and make load transfers.
  • 5. Specify individual capacities per ship.
  • 6. Manage trucks and their loads.
  • 7. Restrict offloading from trucks to the last load.

Bonus Features:

Web-based UI for fleet presentation.

Future Work:

For the API:

  • Add transactions to ensure atomicity.
  • Add locking/semaphores to avoid race conditions when managing containers or changing names.
  • Utilize in-memory cache (e.g., IMemoryCache for single-container applications; for multi-container, consider using Redis) to cache frequently used responses or responses that are expensive and yet rarely changed.
  • Caching can also be performed on the client side for enhanced performance.

Introduce SignalR:

  • Ensure synchronization of all fleet operations throughout all users.
  • For example, when one user loads a container to a ship, it should be visible to other users as well.
